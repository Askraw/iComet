Venue

The iCOMET 2019 will be held at Wanda Realm in Hefei, China. Wanda Realm Hefei is a luxury resort with Hui style architectures. The hotel is located in Hefei Wanda Cultural Tourism City on the Chao Lakeside, next to the Wanda Theme park, Wanda Water Park, Wanda Movie Park, Wanda Mall and Bar Street. It includes fine dining, ballroom, comfortable guestrooms and pool & fitness facilities.

Hefei

Hefei is the capital city of Anhui Province in eastern China. It is an important transportation hub in the area and the famous Mt. Huang (Yellow Mountains) is 300 kilometers away which can be reached conveniently. The beautiful Chao Lake, as the fifth largest freshwater lake of China lies in the southeastern of the city. Hefei is also known as a scientific and educational city where the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and the branches of the Chinese Academy of Sciences locate.

Travel

Situated in the central-east of the country, Hefei is easily reached by plane or by train.

By plane: Hefei Xinqiao International Airport (HFE) is the main airport which connects to most of major cities in China.

By train: Hefei has two major railway stations, Hefei Station and Hefei South Station. It takes only two hours to get to Shanghai by high-speed train and three hours to get to Beijing.

The hotel is 53km from Hefei Xinqiao International Airport and 17 km from the Hefei South Station. You can get to the hotel by bus or taxi.
