Registration information

Registration opens on March 1, 2019. Registration can be made by sending your filled registration form(English Version or Chinese Version) to icomet2019@ustc.edu.cn.

Registration fee

The conference fee covers: a book of abstracts; reception on Sunday evening, tea breaks, lunches, dinners (from Monday to Friday Afternoon), conference excursion and banquet.

All students will receive 150 dollar discount in registration fee to encourage student participation.

Participant Type Registration before June 15, 2019 Registration after June 16, 2019
Regular Fee USD 550 USD 700
Student Fee USD 400 USD 550
Accompany Person Fee USD 300 USD 300

Letter of Invitation

After registration, a letter of invitation will be sent to you together with the confirmation of registration via email. Those who need printed invitation letters for visa applications or other purposes may write to the Conference Secretariat icomet2019@ustc.edu.cn.
