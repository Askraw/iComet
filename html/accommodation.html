Accommodation

The iCOMET 2019 will be held at Wanda Realm Hefei which we recommend as the on-site accommodation. We have booked rooms for all confirmed invited speakers. In addition, we have also negotiated discounted room rates and secured a limited number of guest rooms to make your stay in Hefei affordable and convenient. Rooms at the group rate are limited and subject to availability on a first come, first-served basis.

Besides, there are also other hotels within walking distance. Including:

Wanda Vista Hefei

Pullman Hefei Wanda

Mercure Hefei Wanda

Novotel Hefei Wanda

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us: icomet 2019@ustc.edu.cn
